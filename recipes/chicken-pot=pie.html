Chicken Pot Pie

Home
  • 1lb. chicken
  • 4 potatoes
  • carrots
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 2 pie crusts
  • chicken stock
  • corn starch
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Cube chicken
  3. Place pie crust into bottom of pie pan
  4. Bring Stock to a simmer, and add starch
  5. Add vegetables and chicken to stock
  6. Pour mixture into prepared pie pan
  7. Cover with second pie crust, and seal closed
  8. Vent crust
  9. Place in oven and bake for 55 minutes, or crust is golden brown
  10. Engoy <3
